Yallashop

Ecommerce Application

YallaShop allows you to shop online for your perfect gadget, from mobile phones and TVs, to laptops and digital cameras, to home appliances, and more. Located in Dubai, YallaShop provides a leading system in delivering your electronics needs within the UAE. YallaShop offers its web store via mobile app to enhance customer’s experience, faster checkouts, free shipping, fast delivery, and the best prices. YallaShop also comes with cash or card on delivery methods, warranty, and repairs, to ensure your best choice of shopping online.

List of task performed in developing Yallashop is as follow:
